About Us

Come help us build the education the world deserves

Curly Brace is an education company. But not one in the way you might think. We’re committed to building the best learning experience inside and out, making Curly Brace the best place for our team to learn, teach, and create the online and on-campus learning experience of the future.

Education is old. The current education system is dates back to the 19th century and wasn’t designed to scale the way it has.We are rethinking education from the bottom up.

The web has rethought nearly everything - commerce, social networking, healthcare, and more. We are building the education the world needs - the first truly net native education. We take more cues from modern tech innovators in creating an engaging educational experience than we do from the classroom.

The earnings that comes from curlybrace.org are fully utilized to help the students who are lacking education due to the cost of payment they can't afford to.

Education is broken. Come help us build the education the world deserves.

Our Vision

We envision a world in which everyone can pursue a quality education that enables them to reach their full potential and contribute to their communities and the world.

Our Mission

Increase access to high-quality education for everyone, everywhere. Enhance teaching and learning on campus and online. Advance teaching and learning through research.

Who we are

We are a non-profit organization provides universal access to the world’s best education, partnering with top universities and organizations.

Our goals and metrics

Increase access to high-quality education for everyone, everywhere.
Enhance teaching and learning on campus and online.

Improve diversity in education

The more diverse a faculty, the more diverse the course content, curricular, teaching methods and scholarly ideas presented to students.

Inspire students

Much of what students learn from their greatest teachers is not detailed on a syllabus. Teachers who help us grow as people are responsible for imparting some of life’s most important lessons.

Reach classrooms

Our nationwide network of educators, travelers and volunteers are building a more thoughtful and welcoming society by sharing the eye-opening benefits of travel with the youth of today.

Prep new teachers

Collaboration can help teachers avoid isolation and find sources of support.A personal learning network can help connect teachers to mentors, resources, and support.

Change curriculum

The current education system is dates back to the 19th century and wasn’t designed to scale the way it has.As Knowledge Demands Change, Curriculum Must Change, Too.

Go global

All youth need a global network to help them activate their own global journeys through work and life. All schools need to prepare students for a world where global collaboration is the norm.
